Smoky BBQ Chicken Tacos

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3
Ingredients Quantity
Grilled chicken 2 breasts
Corn tortillas 4
BBQ sauce 1/4 cup
Red onion (thinly sliced) 1/2
Cilantro 1/4 cup
Shredded cheddar cheese 1/2 cup
Lime wedges 2
Sour cream (optional) As desired

Cooking Steps:

  • Step 1: Grill the chicken breasts and coat them with BBQ sauce.
  • Step 2: Warm the corn tortillas on a skillet.
  • Step 3: Slice the grilled chicken and place on tortillas.
  • Step 4: Add red onion, cilantro, and shredded cheese on top. Serve with lime wedges and sour cream.

Mediterranean Falafel Bowl

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Falafel patties 4
Hummus 1/2 cup
Cucumber (diced) 1/2
Cherry tomatoes (halved) 1/4 cup
Red onion (sliced) 1/4
Kalamata olives 1/4 cup
Feta cheese (crumbled) 1/4 cup
Quinoa 1 cup
Tzatziki sauce As desired

Cooking Steps:

  • Step 1: Cook quinoa according to package directions.
  • Step 2: Assemble bowl by adding quinoa, falafel, hummus, and fresh veggies.
  • Step 3: Drizzle with tzatziki sauce and sprinkle feta cheese and olives on top.

Creamy Mushroom Risotto

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Arborio rice 1 cup
Vegetable broth 2 cups
Mushrooms (sliced) 1 cup
Parmesan cheese 1/4 cup
Onion (chopped) 1/2
Butter 2 tbsp
White wine (optional) 1/4 cup
Salt and pepper To taste

Cooking Steps:

  • Step 1: In a pot, heat vegetable broth and keep warm.
  • Step 2: In another pan, saut√© mushrooms and onions in butter until tender.
  • Step 3: Add rice and stir to coat. Slowly add warm broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently.
  • Step 4: Once rice is creamy and tender, stir in Parmesan cheese and white wine, if using. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
