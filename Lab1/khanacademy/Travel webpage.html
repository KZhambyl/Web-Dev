Travel to Norway

Unbelieveable landscapes

Norway is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, where majestic fjords, towering mountains, and pristine lakes create a dramatic and picturesque backdrop. The country’s coastline is dotted with rugged cliffs, while inland, dense forests and snow-capped peaks dominate the scenery. From the stunning Lofoten Islands to the iconic Geirangerfjord, Norway offers a stunning natural beauty that captivates travelers year-round. The Northern Lights also paint the sky in vibrant colors during winter, adding to the allure of this Scandinavian gem.

Amazing animals

Norway is home to a diverse range of wildlife, with species adapted to its varied landscapes, from coastal areas to rugged mountains and dense forests. Iconic animals include the wild reindeer, which roam the northern regions, and the mighty moose, often spotted in forests. Norway's fjords are home to seals, otters, and various seabirds like puffins and eagles. The country also boasts a rich marine life, including whales such as orcas and humpback whales, which migrate along the coast. In the mountainous areas, predators like the elusive lynx and the rare wolverine can be found. Norway's natural habitats are a sanctuary for both common and rare species, making it a haven for wildlife enthusiasts.

Unique kitchen

Norwegian cuisine is centered around fresh, local ingredients like fish, meat, and dairy.

  • Gravlaks (cured salmon)
  • Klippfisk (dried cod)
  • Raspeballer (potato dumplings)
  • Kjøttkaker (meatballs)
  • Brunost (brown cheese)
  • Krumkake (waffle cookies)
  • Bløtkake (cream cake)
  • Rakfisk (fermented fish)
  • Pølse med lompe (sausage with flatbread)
  • Lutefisk (dried fish reconstituted in lye)

Travel plan

  1. Visit the Northern Lights in Tromsø
  2. Explore the stunning fjords, such as Geirangerfjord
  3. Hike to the iconic Preikestolen (Pulpit Rock)
  4. Discover the beauty of the Lofoten Islands
  5. Visit the historic city of Bergen
  6. Go dog sledding in the Arctic region
  7. Experience the midnight sun in the summer months
  8. Take a scenic train ride on the Flåm Railway
  9. Explore the Viking Ship Museum in Oslo
  10. Enjoy a cruise along the Norwegian coast
